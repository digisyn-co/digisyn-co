About Digisyn Co.

We're the studio that stays.

Most agencies build a website and disappear. We were started in 2019 to do the opposite — quietly maintain the websites and online presence of founder-led businesses, year after year.

The studio

A small team across three time zones.

Digisyn is a team of writers, designers, and developers spread across Sydney, Boston, and Calgary. We currently look after about 203 websites — small enough that every client has a senior point of contact, never a junior account manager.

We don't outsource to networks of freelancers. We don't pass off AI-generated copy as written work. Every page we ship is written, edited, and approved by a person on our team.

The goal has always been the same: be the agency a small business can hand the entire website to and stop thinking about.

What we believe

Five things we hold to. Plainly.

Not values written by a brand consultant. Just the things we actually argue about in Slack.

Content is infrastructure

It's the roof, not the lampshade. It needs maintaining, not redesigning every two years.

Boring beats clever

The work that ranks is mostly unglamorous: page speed, internal links, schema, fresh copy. We do that work first.

Monthly > quarterly

A website that's updated every month outranks one that's redesigned every year. Cadence matters more than craft.

One point of contact

No account managers handing you to project managers handing you to juniors. One senior person, for as long as you're with us.

No lock-ins

The Content Care Plan is month-to-month. We earn the renewal by doing the work, not by writing it into a contract.

Plain English reports

One page a month, in your inbox, in language you can read over coffee. No dashboards. No portals. No theatre.
