Privacy Policy

Last updated · January 2026

Digisyn Co. ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. It applies to digisyn.co and all the services we provide through it.

Information we collect

We collect information you give us directly — through contact forms, audit requests, or email — and a small amount of analytics data when you browse the site.

Information you provide

  • Name, business name, email, and website URL when you request an audit
  • Any context you choose to include in your message
  • Billing information if you become a client (held by our payment processor, not by us)

Information collected automatically

  • Pages visited, time on site, referral source (via Ahrefs Analytics)
  • Approximate location, browser, and device type
  • No personally identifying behavioural tracking. No cross-site fingerprinting.

How we use it

We use the information you give us to reply to your enquiry, prepare your audit, and — if you become a client — deliver the service. We use analytics to understand what content works so we can write better pages.

We don't sell your information. We don't share it with advertising networks. We don't run retargeting pixels.

Cookies

We use a single first-party analytics cookie (Ahrefs). It doesn't follow you across sites. You can block it in your browser settings without affecting site functionality.

Your rights

You can ask us, at any time, what information we hold about you, and we'll send it within 30 days. You can ask us to correct or delete it. Email hello@digisyn.co and we'll handle it.

If you're in the EU or UK, you have rights under GDPR. If you're in California, you have rights under CCPA. We respect both, and the easiest path is to email us.

How long we keep it

Audit requests that don't convert are deleted after 12 months. Client records are kept for the duration of the engagement plus seven years for tax purposes. Analytics data is retained for 14 months.

Changes to this policy

We update this page when our practices change. Material changes will be announced to active clients by email. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy can go to hello@digisyn.co. We'll reply within two working days.
