Pricing questions, answered honestly.

Why don't you charge per hour?

Hourly billing rewards slow work and punishes efficiency. Monthly plans mean we're motivated to deliver value consistently, not to clock hours.

What if I need more than my plan includes?

We'll tell you before going over scope. You can approve extra work at a pre-agreed rate, upgrade your plan, or wait until next month. No surprise invoices.

Do I need a new website to work with you?

No. Most clients come to us with an existing site. We work with what you have, fix urgent issues first, and improve it over time.

How is Digisyn Co. different from a freelancer?

Freelancers are individuals. We are a small in-house team — writers, designers, and developers. No subcontracting, no outsourcing. The same people work on your account every month.

What happens if I want to cancel?

Send us an email. Your plan ends at the next billing date. No notice period, no cancellation fee, no awkward exit process.

Is the free audit really free?

Yes. It is a real written audit — not a template, not an automated report. We offer it because the people who see the quality of our thinking almost always become clients.
